## 📌 Project Overview

An **Advanced** retail analytics project simulating real-world data science
and strategic consulting work at **Quantium**.

The objective: ingest, clean, and analyze **260K+ retail transactions**,
conduct statistical A/B testing on store layouts, and deliver
actionable **C-level commercial recommendations**.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- Which customer segments drive the most revenue?
- Did the new store layout trial significantly increase sales?
- What commercial strategy should Quantium pursue for the next half-year?

---

## 🔍 Key Strategic Findings

| Metric | Detail | Business Impact |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Top Revenue Segment** | Older Families - Budget ($168K+) | Protect via bulk promotions |
| **Highest Price Per Unit** | Young Singles/Couples - Mainstream ($4.08) | Target for premium merchandising |
| **Trial Store 77 Uplift** | **+62%** vs control store | Statistically significant success |
| **Final Recommendation** | National Layout Rollout | Scale across all retail branches |
